At VoltVandal iPhone Lab, we stand behind every repair we perform. When you leave our shop, your device is covered by a **90-day warranty on all parts and labor** — no registration required. If something goes wrong as a result of our work within that window, we'll make it right at no additional charge.

## What's Covered

Your 90-day warranty applies to any issue that traces back directly to the repair we performed for you.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Parts Defects">
    If a replacement part — screen, battery, camera module, charging port, or any other component we installed — fails due to a manufacturing defect, we'll replace it free of charge within 90 days of your repair date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Labor Defects">
    If a problem arises because of how the repair was performed — a loose connector, an improperly seated component, or any workmanship issue — we'll re-open the device and correct it at no cost to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Part Failure Unrelated to Misuse">
    If a replaced part stops functioning correctly under normal use within the warranty period, you're covered. Bring the device back in and we'll diagnose and replace the faulty component.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Functionality Issues Tied to the Repair">
    If a feature directly associated with your repair — such as Face ID after a screen replacement or charging speed after a port repair — isn't working as expected, that falls under warranty and we'll address it at no charge.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What's Not Covered

<Warning>
  The following situations are **excluded** from warranty coverage:

  - **Physical damage after repair** — cracked screens, bent frames, or any damage that occurred after you left our shop
  - **Liquid or water damage after repair** — exposure to moisture following the repair date is not covered
  - **Software issues** — iOS bugs, app crashes, or any problem that isn't hardware-related
  - **Damage caused by unauthorized repairs** — if another shop or individual opens or modifies the device after VoltVandal performed the covered repair, the warranty is void
  - **Normal wear and tear** — battery capacity degradation over time, minor cosmetic wear, and similar aging are expected and not covered
</Warning>

## How to File a Warranty Claim

Filing a claim is straightforward. Here's what to do:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact VoltVandal">
    Reach out to us by phone, email, or through the contact form at [/support/contact](/support/contact). Let us know the date of your original repair and describe the issue you're experiencing. Have your repair receipt or order number handy if possible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Issue">
    Tell us exactly what's happening — when the problem started, how often it occurs, and which function is affected. The more detail you provide, the faster we can confirm warranty eligibility and prepare for your visit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bring In Your Device for Inspection">
    Visit any VoltVandal location (or ship your device for mail-in claims). A technician will inspect the repair and confirm whether the issue falls under warranty. If it does, we'll begin the fix immediately — most warranty repairs are completed the same day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Warranty Repair Turnaround

Warranty repairs follow the same priority queue as standard repairs. In most cases, your device will be ready **same day**, often within a few hours. If a part needs to be ordered, we'll give you an updated estimate up front.

<Note>
  Your warranty is **non-transferable** and tied to the original repair on your specific device. It applies only to the person who authorized the repair and cannot be extended, transferred to a new owner, or applied to a different device.
</Note>
