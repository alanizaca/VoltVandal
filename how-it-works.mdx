At VoltVandal iPhone Lab, we believe repairs should be fast, transparent, and stress-free. Every device that comes through our door — or arrives by mail — goes through the same careful diagnostic and repair process. You'll always know what's wrong, what it costs, and exactly when to expect your phone back before any work begins.

## The Repair Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Device">
    Drop off your iPhone in person at our lab or ship it to us using our mail-in service. Either way, your device is logged into our system and assigned to a certified technician as soon as it arrives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Free Diagnostic Assessment">
    Your technician runs a full diagnostic on your device at no charge. We inspect the reported issue as well as the overall health of your iPhone — screen, battery, cameras, ports, and more — so nothing gets missed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the Quote">
    Once the diagnostic is complete, we send you a clear, itemized quote covering parts and labor. There's no pressure and no hidden fees. If you decide not to move forward, you owe us nothing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repair Performed">
    After you approve the quote, your technician gets to work using quality replacement parts. Common repairs like screen and battery replacements are typically completed the same day, while more complex issues take 1–3 business days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Up or Receive Your Device">
    Walk-in customers are notified the moment their repair is done and can collect their iPhone at the lab. Mail-in customers receive their device back via return shipping, fully repaired and protected for transit. All repairs are backed by our 90-day warranty on parts and labor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Walk-In vs. Mail-In

Whether you're local or across the country, VoltVandal has a repair path that works for you.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Walk-In">
    Walking in is the fastest way to get your iPhone repaired. Bring your device to our lab during business hours and we'll take care of everything on the spot.

    **What to bring:**
    - Your iPhone (powered off if possible)
    - Your passcode — technicians may need access to verify the repair
    - A valid photo ID for device check-in

    **What to expect on-site:**
    Once you arrive, a technician will check in your device and begin the free diagnostic right away. For common repairs like screen or battery replacements, you can often wait in-store or step out and return the same day. We'll text or call you as soon as your iPhone is ready for pick-up.

    <Tip>
      Same-day turnaround is available for most screen and battery replacements when you walk in before 5 PM.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mail-In">
    Can't make it in person? Ship your iPhone to us and we'll repair it and send it right back to you.

    **Packaging instructions:**
    - Power off your device before packing it
    - Wrap your iPhone in bubble wrap or foam padding
    - Place it in a rigid box with at least one inch of padding on all sides
    - Include a note with your name, contact number, and a brief description of the issue

    **Shipping tips:**
    - Use a carrier that provides a tracking number (UPS, FedEx, or USPS Priority Mail recommended)
    - Consider purchasing shipping insurance for your device's full value
    - Email us your tracking number at the time of shipment so we can watch for your arrival

    **Return shipping:**
    Once your repair is complete, we'll ship your iPhone back using the same carrier class you used to send it. You'll receive a tracking number by email or text as soon as your package leaves our lab.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Diagnostics are completely free — we never charge you just to look at your device. If you decide not to proceed with a repair after receiving your quote, your iPhone will be returned to you at no cost.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Most screen and battery replacements are completed the same day. Walk in before 5 PM and you'll typically leave with a fully repaired iPhone.
</Tip>
