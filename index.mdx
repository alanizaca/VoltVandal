## How It Works

Getting your iPhone repaired at VoltVandal is simple. Here's what to expect from start to finish.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Issue">
    Tell us what's going on with your iPhone — in store, over the phone, or through our online booking form. Not sure what's wrong? No problem. We offer **free diagnostics** on every device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drop Off or Mail In Your Device">
    Bring your iPhone to our lab for a same-day walk-in repair, or use our convenient mail-in program to ship your device directly to us from anywhere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get It Back, Fixed">
    Once your repair is complete, pick up your device in store or have it shipped back to you. Most repairs are finished the same day — ready when you need it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Every repair at VoltVandal iPhone Lab is covered by a **90-day warranty**. If you experience any issues related to your repair within 90 days, bring your device back and we'll fix it at no extra cost.
</Tip>
