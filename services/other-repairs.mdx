Screen and battery repairs get most of the attention, but a broken camera, a charging port that won't connect, or a side button that stopped clicking can be just as disruptive to your daily life. VoltVandal iPhone Lab handles the full range of iPhone hardware repairs — all backed by free diagnostics, OEM-grade parts, and a 90-day warranty on every job.

<Note>
  Not sure what's wrong with your iPhone? Every repair at VoltVandal starts with a **free diagnostic**. Bring your device in and we'll identify the issue before you commit to anything — no charge, no obligation.
</Note>

## Repair Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Camera Repair">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Photos are blurry or out of focus even after cleaning the lens
    - The rear or front camera shows a black screen when opened
    - The camera app crashes or freezes on launch
    - Flash stops working or fires at the wrong intensity
    - Portrait mode, zoom, or wide-angle lenses behave erratically

    **What we do:**  
    Our technicians inspect both the front (TrueDepth) and rear camera modules, test autofocus and optical image stabilization, and replace the faulty module with an OEM-grade part matched to your iPhone model. Lens alignment and camera app functionality are verified before your device is returned.

    **Typical turnaround:** Same day for most models; 1–2 business days if parts need to be ordered for older models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Charging Port Repair">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Your iPhone won't charge even with a known-good cable and adapter
    - The cable feels loose or falls out when plugged in
    - Charging is intermittent — it connects and disconnects repeatedly
    - Lint, debris, or visible damage is present inside the Lightning or USB-C port
    - Your computer doesn't recognize the device when connected

    **What we do:**  
    We begin by safely clearing any debris from the port — in many cases, a thorough cleaning resolves the issue without a full replacement. If the port itself is damaged, we replace the entire charging assembly with an OEM-grade component and verify charging and data transfer before returning your device.

    **Typical turnaround:** Same day for most models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Speaker & Microphone Repair">
    **Symptoms:**
    - The person on the other end of calls can't hear you clearly
    - Your iPhone's speaker sounds muffled, distorted, or produces no audio
    - Siri consistently fails to understand you or says it can't hear you
    - Speakerphone doesn't work during calls
    - Videos you record have poor or no audio

    **What we do:**  
    We test all audio components — the earpiece speaker, bottom-firing speaker, and all microphones — to isolate the fault. Affected components are replaced with OEM-grade parts, and full audio testing is performed across calls, media playback, and voice input before handoff.

    **Typical turnaround:** Same day for most models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Home Button & Side Button Repair">
    **Symptoms:**
    - The Home button (iPhone 8 and earlier) is unresponsive or requires excessive force
    - The Side/Power button is stuck, sunken, or won't register a press
    - Volume buttons feel loose, rattle, or don't respond
    - Face ID isn't working as expected on iPhone X and later
    - Your iPhone won't wake from sleep or restart properly

    **What we do:**  
    Button mechanisms are inspected for physical damage, debris, or connector issues. We replace faulty button assemblies with OEM-grade components. Note that Home button Touch ID functionality on iPhone 7 and 8 is paired to the original logic board — a third-party replacement can restore the physical click but will not restore Touch ID. We'll explain your options clearly during the free diagnostic.

    **Typical turnaround:** Same day for most models; 1–2 business days for Face ID module repairs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Back Glass Repair">
    **Symptoms:**
    - The rear glass panel is cracked or shattered (iPhone 8 and later)
    - Sharp glass edges are a safety hazard
    - Cracks are spreading toward the camera module or Apple logo cutout
    - Your case no longer fits properly due to raised or missing glass

    **What we do:**  
    Starting with the iPhone 8, Apple moved to a glass back panel — and while it looks great, it's fragile. We use laser removal equipment to safely detach the broken glass and bond a new OEM-grade rear panel in place. The repair preserves your device's wireless charging capability and water resistance seals where applicable.

    **Typical turnaround:** 1–2 business days depending on the model and color availability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Warranty

Every repair performed at VoltVandal — regardless of the service type — comes with a **90-day warranty** covering both parts and labor. If a repaired component fails due to a defect in the part or our workmanship within 90 days of your repair, we'll fix it at no additional cost. The warranty does not cover new damage caused by drops, liquid exposure, or other external factors after your repair is complete.

To make a claim, return to the shop with your original repair receipt. You can also review full coverage details and the claim process on our [Warranty page](/warranty).
