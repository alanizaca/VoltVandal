Most repairs are completed the same day you walk in.

## Signs You Need a Screen Repair

If your iPhone is showing any of the following symptoms, a screen replacement is likely the right fix:

- **Cracked or shattered glass** — visible fractures across the front panel, even hairline cracks that worsen over time
- **Touch not responding** — taps, swipes, or gestures are ignored or register in the wrong location
- **Black screen** — the display stays dark even though the phone powers on (you can feel vibrations or hear sounds)
- **Display lines or discoloration** — vertical or horizontal lines, green tinting, or blotchy patches on the screen
- **Brightness or flickering issues** — the screen dims unexpectedly, flickers, or won't reach full brightness

## Warranty

Every screen repair at VoltVandal is covered by a **90-day warranty** on both parts and labor. If your replacement screen develops a defect — such as a touch sensitivity issue, unexpected discoloration, or display failure — within 90 days of your repair, we'll fix it at no additional cost. The warranty does not cover new physical damage, such as a subsequent drop or crack.

To make a warranty claim, bring your device back to the shop with your original repair receipt. You can also review the full warranty terms and claim process on our [Warranty page](/warranty).
