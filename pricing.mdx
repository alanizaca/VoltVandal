## Payment Methods

We accept all major payment options so you can pay however is most convenient for you:

- **Credit & Debit Cards** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Apple Pay** — tap to pay at the counter
- **Venmo** — send payment from your phone
- **Cash** — always welcome

## Mail-In Repairs

You don't have to come to us. VoltVandal accepts mail-in repairs for customers across the country.

<Tip>
  Mail-in repair pricing is identical to walk-in pricing. We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label once your repair is complete — return shipping is included at no extra charge. Pack your device securely and ship it to the address provided at booking.
</Tip>
