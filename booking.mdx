Booking a repair at VoltVandal iPhone Lab is quick and straightforward. You can walk in any day during business hours, schedule an appointment in advance, or ship your device to us from anywhere. No matter which option you choose, you'll receive a free diagnostic and a clear quote before a single repair is made.

## How to Book Your Repair

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Service">
    Select the repair you need — screen replacement, battery replacement, camera repair, charging port repair, water damage recovery, button repair, speaker or microphone repair, or a general diagnostic. Not sure what's wrong? A free diagnostic will identify the issue for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Your Device Information">
    Let us know your iPhone model (iPhone 6 through iPhone 15 series) and describe the issue you're experiencing. The more detail you provide, the faster we can prepare for your repair and give you an accurate time estimate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Walk-In or Mail-In">
    Choose whether you'll bring your device to our lab in person or ship it to us. Walk-in customers can arrive during business hours with no appointment required, though scheduling ahead guarantees your preferred time slot. Mail-in customers will receive packing and shipping instructions by email after booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Booking">
    Review your details and confirm. You'll receive a confirmation by email or text with everything you need — your appointment time, our lab address, or your mail-in shipping instructions. We'll be ready for your device when you arrive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Have Ready

Having the right information on hand makes check-in faster and helps your technician get started right away.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Your iPhone Model">
    Know which iPhone model you have — for example, iPhone 13 Pro, iPhone 15, or iPhone SE (3rd generation). You can find this under **Settings → General → About**. We service all models from iPhone 6 through the full iPhone 15 series.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A Description of the Issue">
    Describe what's wrong as clearly as you can. Is the screen cracked or unresponsive? Is the battery draining unusually fast? Did the phone get wet? The more detail you provide, the more efficiently we can run your diagnostic and quote the repair.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your Device Passcode">
    Technicians may need access to your iPhone to verify that the repair was successful — for example, confirming touch responsiveness after a screen replacement. Have your passcode ready at check-in. You are welcome to change it after your repair is complete.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your Contact Information">
    Provide a phone number or email address where we can reach you with your quote and repair status updates. For mail-in repairs, we'll also use this to send your return tracking number.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
