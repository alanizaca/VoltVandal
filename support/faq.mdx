Whether it's your first time visiting us or you're a returning customer, you probably have a few questions before handing over your iPhone. We've compiled answers to the most common things people ask about our repair process, parts, pricing, and policies — so you can walk in (or mail in) with full confidence.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="How long does a repair take?">
    Most common repairs — screen replacements, battery swaps, and charging port fixes — are completed **same day**, often within 1–2 hours while you wait or run errands. More complex repairs such as logic board work or water damage recovery typically take **1–3 business days**. You'll receive a time estimate after your free diagnostic, so there are no surprises.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you use genuine Apple parts?">
    We use **high-quality OEM-equivalent and premium aftermarket parts** that meet or exceed Apple's performance specifications. For certain repairs, we also offer genuine Apple parts when available. During your diagnostic, our technician will walk you through your parts options and any differences in price or performance so you can make the best choice for your device and budget.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my data be safe during repair?">
    Your data is your business — not ours. Our technicians are trained to perform repairs without accessing your personal files, photos, or apps. That said, we strongly recommend **backing up your iPhone** before any repair as a precaution. You can back up via iCloud (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup) or via your computer using Finder or iTunes. We are not responsible for data loss resulting from pre-existing device damage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my repair fails or the problem returns?">
    Every repair performed at VoltVandal is covered by our **90-day warranty on parts and labor**. If the same issue returns within 90 days of your repair, bring your iPhone back and we'll make it right at no additional cost. This warranty covers defects in our workmanship and parts — it does not cover new physical damage or unrelated issues that arise after the repair.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer a warranty?">
    Yes. All repairs come with a **90-day warranty** covering both parts and labor. This means if the component we replaced fails or our repair doesn't hold up within 90 days, we'll fix it for free. Simply bring in your iPhone along with your original receipt, and a technician will assess and resolve the issue promptly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I mail in my iPhone for repair?">
    Absolutely. Our **mail-in repair service** is available nationwide. Here's how it works:

    1. Contact us at [support@voltvandal.com](mailto:support@voltvandal.com) or text **(555) 867-5309** to describe your issue and get a quote.
    2. Pack your iPhone securely in a padded envelope or small box and ship it to **1247 Volt Ave, Suite 3, Los Angeles, CA 90012**.
    3. We'll perform a free diagnostic, confirm the repair scope and cost with you, then complete the work.
    4. Your repaired iPhone is shipped back to you, typically within 2–5 business days of receipt.

    We recommend using a trackable shipping method and purchasing insurance for your shipment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does a repair cost?">
    Repair pricing varies depending on your **iPhone model** and the **type of repair** needed. Screen replacements, battery replacements, and charging port repairs are among our most common and competitively priced services. The best way to get an accurate quote is to:

    - **Text or call us** at (555) 867-5309 with your iPhone model and issue.
    - **Walk in** for a free diagnostic — no appointment needed.
    - **Email us** at support@voltvandal.com for a mail-in estimate.

    We'll always provide a full quote before any work begins, so you're never hit with unexpected charges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you repair iPhones with cracked back glass?">
    Yes! Back glass repair is one of our specialties. Cracked back glass is a common issue — especially on iPhone 8 through iPhone 15 Pro Max — and we have the tools and expertise to replace it cleanly. Turnaround is typically **same day** for most models. Contact us with your iPhone model for a specific quote.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can you repair water-damaged iPhones?">
    We can and we do. Water damage is one of the more complex repairs, but our technicians are experienced in **liquid damage assessment and board-level cleaning**. If your iPhone has gotten wet, the most important thing you can do is **power it off immediately and bring it in as soon as possible** — the longer liquid sits inside, the more damage it can cause.

    Water damage repairs typically take **1–3 business days** depending on severity. Keep in mind that heavily corroded devices may not be fully recoverable, but we'll always give you an honest assessment after the free diagnostic.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major forms of payment, including:

    - **Credit cards** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express
    - **Apple Pay** — tap to pay at the counter
    - **Cash** — always welcome

    Payment is collected upon completion of the repair for walk-in customers, and confirmed prior to return shipping for mail-in customers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need an appointment for walk-in repairs?">
    No appointment is necessary — we welcome **walk-ins anytime during business hours**:

    - **Monday–Saturday:** 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
    - **Sunday:** 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM

    That said, if you'd like to guarantee a specific time slot or have a complex repair, you're welcome to book ahead. Appointments can be requested via our booking page or by texting us at (555) 867-5309.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What iPhone models do you service?">
    We repair **all iPhone models from iPhone 6 through iPhone 15 Pro Max**, including:

    - iPhone 6, 6 Plus, 6s, 6s Plus
    - iPhone 7, 7 Plus
    - iPhone 8, 8 Plus
    - iPhone X, XR, XS, XS Max
    - iPhone 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max
    - iPhone 12, 12 Mini, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max
    - iPhone 13, 13 Mini, 13 Pro, 13 Pro Max
    - iPhone 14, 14 Plus, 14 Pro, 14 Pro Max
    - iPhone 15, 15 Plus, 15 Pro, 15 Pro Max

    Not sure if your model is covered? Just reach out — we're happy to confirm before you come in.
  </Accordion>
