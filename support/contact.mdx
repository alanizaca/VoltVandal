## Mail-In Repair Instructions

Can't make it to our shop in person? Our mail-in repair service is available to customers throughout the United States. Here's how to send your iPhone to us:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact us first to confirm scope and pricing">
    Before shipping anything, reach out by text at **(555) 867-5309** or email **support@voltvandal.com** with your iPhone model and a description of the issue. We'll confirm whether the repair is something we can handle by mail and provide an estimated quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pack your iPhone securely">
    Wrap your iPhone in bubble wrap or foam padding and place it inside a small, sturdy box. Do **not** include your charger, case, or accessories unless specifically instructed — just the bare device. Seal the box securely with packing tape.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship to our address with tracking and insurance">
    Send your package to:

    **VoltVandal iPhone Lab**
    1247 Volt Ave, Suite 3
    Los Angeles, CA 90012

    Use a trackable shipping method (USPS Priority Mail, UPS, or FedEx) and purchase shipping insurance for the full value of your device. Keep your tracking number — we'll let you know when your package arrives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We'll diagnose, confirm, and repair">
    Once your iPhone arrives, we'll run our free diagnostic and reach out to confirm the final repair cost and timeline before doing any work. After your approval, we'll complete the repair and ship your device back via a trackable method, typically within **2–5 business days** of receipt.
  </Step>
</Steps>
